# Kubota Tractors (NEW — always offer these first)

## Kubota B2441 — 24 HP compact tractor, 4WD (B-series)
- ~24 HP 3-cyl diesel, 4WD, power steering, narrow width — fits between
  coconut / oil-palm / banana / cocoa / vegetable rows. Light footprint
  protects orchard soil. Implements: rotavator, cultivator, sprayer,
  small trailer.
- Solves: big tractors can't enter orchard rows; monthly weeding labour
  cost; soil compaction near tree roots; oversized diesel bills.
- తెలుగు: తోటల్లో వరుసల మధ్య వెళ్లే చిన్న 4WD ట్రాక్టర్. కొబ్బరి, ఆయిల్
  పామ్, అరటి రైతులకు. తక్కువ డీజిల్.

## Kubota B2741 — 27 HP compact tractor, 4WD (B-series)
- B2441's stronger brother: same compact size, more torque for harder
  rotavation and hauling loaded trailers in orchards. 4WD, power
  steering. Implements: rotavator, small MB plough, trailer, sprayer.
- Solves: compact tractors stalling in hard dry summer soil; hauling
  harvest from garden to road; one machine for spray/weed/rotavate/haul.
- తెలుగు: B2441 కంటే బలమైన మోడల్ — గట్టి నేలల్లో రోటావేషన్, నిండిన
  ట్రాలీ లాగుతుంది.

## Kubota B-SERIES range — compact family, ~21 HP upward
- Multiple compact 4WD models beyond B2441/B2741 for orchards,
  vegetables, inter-cultivation. We bring a FREE demo to the customer's
  field to choose the right size.
- తెలుగు: మీ వరుసలకు, బడ్జెట్‌కు ఏ మోడల్ సరిపోతుందో ఉచిత పొల డెమోతో
  చూపిస్తాము.

## Kubota L4508 — 45 HP, puddling (dammu) specialist (L-series)
- ~45 HP 4-cyl Japanese E-CDIS diesel, 4WD, 8F+4R transmission, precise
  hydraulics. Famous for balance in wet-land puddling and low diesel
  use per acre. Strong resale value in AP.
- Solves: short canal-water windows (puddle all acres in time); ordinary
  tractors getting stuck in wet delta soil; season's biggest diesel
  bill; resale value worries.
- తెలుగు: దమ్ము స్పెషలిస్ట్. E-CDIS ఇంజన్‌తో ఎకరాకు డీజిల్ ఆదా, లోతైన
  దమ్ముల్లోనూ నడుస్తుంది. AP‌లో మంచి రీసేల్.

## Kubota MU4201 — 42 HP (MU-series, value model)
- ~42 HP diesel, 2WD/4WD variants, flat deck, side-shift lever. For
  rotavation, cultivation, trailer, thresher.
- Solves: old-tractor breakdowns mid-season; repair bills exceeding an
  EMI; Kubota quality at the most accessible MU price point.
- తెలుగు: తక్కువ ధరలో కుబోటా నాణ్యత. అన్ని పనులకు.

## Kubota MU4501 — 45 HP (MU-series, bestseller)
- Kubota's bestselling tractor in India. ~45 HP 4-cyl diesel, 8F+4R,
  2WD/4WD, high-capacity hydraulic lift, famous fuel economy. Balanced
  for dammu + dry land + haulage — one tractor for everything.
- Solves: owning separate machines for dammu/dry/haulage; diesel as the
  biggest running cost; 45 HP sweet spot (wet-field ability + big
  rotavator lift); strong AP second-hand value.
- తెలుగు: గోదావరి రైతుల ఎంపిక. దమ్ము, మెట్ట, రవాణా — మూడూ ఒకే
  ట్రాక్టర్‌తో. ప్రసిద్ధ డీజిల్ ఆదా.

## Kubota MU5502 — 55 HP (MU-series flagship)
- ~55 HP 4-cyl diesel, 2WD/4WD, synchro shuttle, ergonomic deck. Runs
  heavy implements: laser leveller, big rotavator, baler, loaded
  trailers.
- Solves: laser levelling (saves 20–25% irrigation water) needs power;
  heavy implements stall smaller tractors; premium custom-hiring rates
  for levelling/baling work.
- తెలుగు: బరువైన పరికరాలకు 55 HP ఫ్లాగ్‌షిప్ — లేజర్ లెవలర్, పెద్ద
  రోటావేటర్, బేలర్.
