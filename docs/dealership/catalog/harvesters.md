# Kubota Combine Harvesters

## Kubota DC-68G-HK KING PRO — 68 HP combine harvester (paddy specialist)
- Engine: 4-cylinder turbo diesel, 2,434 cc, ~68 HP. Grain tank: 1,250 L
  (unloads in ~90 seconds, 235° swing auger). Cutter/working width
  ~2,245 mm (6.5 ft class). Fuel tank 60 L. Crawler tracks with HST
  (stepless) drive. Capacity 5.75–10 acres/day.
- Solves: harvest labour gangs cost ₹4,000–6,000/acre and vanish at peak
  season; finishes 8–10 acres a day before cyclones/unseasonal delta
  rain; wide crawlers work in mud and standing water where wheeled
  harvesters sink; one-pass cut+thresh+clean avoids shattering losses.
- Best for: custom-hiring operators and large paddy farmers in wet
  Godavari fields.

  90 సెకన్లలో ఖాళీ. బురద పొలాల్లోనూ పని చేస్తుంది. రోజుకు 5.75–10
  ఎకరాలు. కోత కూలీ ఖర్చు, తుఫాను భయం తగ్గిస్తుంది.

## Kubota DC-99 HARVESKING — 98 HP high-capacity combine harvester
- Engine: 4-cyl liquid-cooled V3800 diesel, 3,769 cc, ~98.3 HP. Grain
  tank 1,800 L. Working width ~2,422 mm. Fuel tank 120 L (full day
  without refuelling). Threshing drum 620×1,960 mm, 6-block concave.
  Kubota 3-way air-stream cleaning. 550 mm crawlers, HST.
- Solves: rental harvester queues at peak (everyone harvests the same 2
  weeks); 1,800 L tank nearly halves unloading stops; earns
  custom-hiring income across villages after your own fields.
- Best for: contractors and operators who want maximum acres per day.
- తెలుగు: కుబోటా పెద్ద యంత్రం — 98 HP, 1,800 లీ. ట్యాంక్, 120 లీ.
  డీజిల్ ట్యాంక్. రోజంతా ఆగకుండా పని. అద్దె వ్యాపారానికి ఉత్తమం.
